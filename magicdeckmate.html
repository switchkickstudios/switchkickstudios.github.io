Magic DeckMate
Magic DeckMate is built to be the most complete and interactive game companion for MTG on the App Store. Other than your actual deck, it is designed to be all a player needs to keep track of everything in game, featuring interactive, touch-controlled virtual tokens and counters. With Magic DeckMate, you can leave your dice and tokens at home, making it perfect for travel. All you need for game night are your cards and your device!
