Magic DeckMate

Leave your dice at home. All you need are your cards and your device.
Magic DeckMate is built to be the world’s most robust in-game Magic: The Gathering companion. Featuring interactive touch-controlled virtual tokens and counters, it is all you need to keep track of everything in-game in the most convenient and visual way possible.
FREE
Perfect for:
  • Travel — This app is an absolute necessity for playing on trains and planes.
  • Being an idiot and forgetting your dice at home.
  • Being a genius and not needing dice to play Magic.
Features

Tokens

  • Every single token in the game (except for some from Unglued and Unhinged)
  • Interactive virtual tokens
    • Swipe left or right across the card to tap or untap
    • Swipe up or down over the card to keep track of the quantity of each token.
    • Press and hold to remove the token from the battlefield.

Life Counter

  • Set your starting life to 20, 30, or 40
  • Easily reset the life counter to the default starting life
